Pros to Pair Programming

Pair programming has many benefits and advantages as compared to solo programming. It allows people to catch errors earlier. Having two people understand the code makes it easier to maintain. Also problem solving can be more dynamic and innovative, because two heads are better than one! Pair programming is important at DBC because it is teaches how to work well with other people and is an important employable skill.

There is a driver and a navigator in each pair. The role of the navigator is to direct the session and give instructions to the driver. The role of the driver is to execute the navigator's directions by typing out code as the navigator specifies.
